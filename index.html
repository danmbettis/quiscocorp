Policies

Refund Policy

Because services involve time and labor, payments are generally non-refundable once work begins. If you cancel before work begins, you may request a refund minus any non-refundable processing fees (if applicable). If we fail to deliver the agreed service and cannot remedy the issue, a partial or full refund may be considered based on work completed.

Refund requests: info@quiscocorp.com

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are permitted before work begins. After work begins, you may request cancellation of remaining work, but refunds are not guaranteed due to time already committed. We will make reasonable efforts to reschedule when possible.

Delivery Policy

Qui’s Co Corp primarily delivers services and digital deliverables via email or secure link. Typical delivery timelines are listed in the package details above. Delays may occur if required client information/approvals are not provided promptly.
